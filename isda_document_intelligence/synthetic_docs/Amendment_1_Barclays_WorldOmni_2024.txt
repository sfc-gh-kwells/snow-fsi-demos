FIRST AMENDMENT
to the
ISDA MASTER AGREEMENT

dated as of March 1, 2024

between

BARCLAYS BANK PLC
("Party A")

and

WORLD OMNI AUTO RECEIVABLES TRUST 2007-B
("Party B")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Party A and Party B are parties to an ISDA Master Agreement dated as of 
September 26, 2007, including the Schedule thereto (collectively, the "Agreement");

WHEREAS, the parties desire to amend certain provisions of the Agreement, including 
provisions relating to Cross Default thresholds and Automatic Early Termination;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual agreements contained herein and for 
other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are 
hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE I
AMENDMENTS TO THE SCHEDULE

Section 1.1 Cross Default Threshold Amount

Part 1(f) of the Schedule to the Agreement is hereby amended and restated in its 
entirety to read as follows:

"(f) Cross Default. The provisions of Section 5(a)(vi) will apply to Party A and 
     will apply to Party B.

     'Specified Indebtedness' means any obligation (whether present or future, 
     contingent or otherwise, as principal or surety or otherwise) in respect of 
     borrowed money.

     'Threshold Amount' means:

     (i) With respect to Party A: USD 10,000,000 (Ten Million United States Dollars)

     (ii) With respect to Party B: USD 5,000,000 (Five Million United States Dollars)

     For the avoidance of doubt, a default, event of default or other similar 
     condition or event in respect of Specified Indebtedness in an aggregate 
     amount equal to or exceeding the applicable Threshold Amount shall constitute 
     a Cross Default under Section 5(a)(vi) of this Agreement."

Section 1.2 Automatic Early Termination

Part 1(h) of the Schedule to the Agreement is hereby amended and restated in its 
entirety to read as follows:

"(h) Automatic Early Termination. The 'Automatic Early Termination' provision of 
     Section 6(a) will apply to Party A and will apply to Party B.

     Notwithstanding the foregoing, Automatic Early Termination shall apply to a 
     party only in respect of an Event of Default specified in Section 5(a)(vii)(1), 
     (3), (4), (5), (6) or, to the extent analogous thereto, (8) of this Agreement, 
     and only if the jurisdiction whose laws govern that party has in effect a 
     system under which:

     (a) contractual termination provisions of the kind set out in Section 6(a) 
         may be rendered ineffective by a filing of, or similar action in, a 
         bankruptcy, insolvency or similar proceeding; or

     (b) a party's rights under Section 6 may be materially impaired by operation 
         of any stay, moratorium or similar order or process."

Section 1.3 Credit Event Upon Merger

Part 1(j) of the Schedule to the Agreement is hereby amended to add the following 
provision:

"Credit Event Upon Merger. The provisions of Section 5(b)(v) will apply to Party A 
and will apply to Party B.

For purposes of Section 5(b)(v), 'materially weaker' means that the creditworthiness 
of the relevant entity is, in the reasonable opinion of the other party, materially 
weaker than that of Party A or Party B, as applicable, immediately prior to such 
action, taking into account any credit support or guarantee that may be provided."

ARTICLE II
ADDITIONAL PROVISIONS

Section 2.1 Set-off Rights

The following provision is hereby added to Part 5 of the Schedule:

"Set-off. Upon the occurrence of an Early Termination Date, either party (X) may 
set off any sum or obligation owed by the other party (Y) to X under this Agreement 
or any Credit Support Document against any sum or obligation owed by X to Y under 
this Agreement, any Credit Support Document, or any other agreement between the 
parties. The obligations of the parties under this provision shall survive the 
termination of this Agreement."

Section 2.2 Recording of Conversations

The following provision is hereby added to Part 5 of the Schedule:

"Recording of Conversations. Each party consents to the recording of telephone 
conversations between the trading, operations, and other relevant personnel of the 
parties in connection with this Agreement or any Transaction."

ARTICLE III
REPRESENTATIONS, WARRANTIES AND EFFECTIVENESS

Section 3.1 Representations and Warranties

Each party represents and warrants to the other that:

(a) It has all necessary power and authority to execute and deliver this Amendment 
    and to perform its obligations hereunder;

(b) This Amendment has been duly authorized, executed and delivered by it and 
    constitutes its legal, valid and binding obligation;

(c) No Event of Default or Potential Event of Default has occurred and is continuing;

(d) All representations and warranties made by it in the Agreement are true and 
    correct as of the date hereof.

Section 3.2 Effectiveness

This Amendment shall become effective as of March 1, 2024 (the "Amendment Effective 
Date").

Section 3.3 Continuity of Agreement

Except as expressly amended hereby, the Agreement shall continue in full force and 
effect in accordance with its terms. On and after the Amendment Effective Date, each 
reference in the Agreement to "this Agreement," "hereunder," "hereof," or words of 
like import shall mean and be a reference to the Agreement as amended by this 
Amendment.

Section 3.4 Governing Law

This Amendment shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of 
the State of New York (without reference to choice of law doctrine).

Section 3.5 Counterparts

This Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an 
original.
