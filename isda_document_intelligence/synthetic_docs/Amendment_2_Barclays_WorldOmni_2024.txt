SECOND AMENDMENT
to the
ISDA MASTER AGREEMENT

dated as of September 15, 2024

between

BARCLAYS BANK PLC
("Party A")

and

WORLD OMNI AUTO RECEIVABLES TRUST 2007-B
("Party B")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Party A and Party B are parties to an ISDA Master Agreement dated as of 
September 26, 2007, including the Schedule thereto, as amended by the First Amendment 
dated as of March 1, 2024 (collectively, the "Agreement");

WHEREAS, the parties now desire to further amend certain provisions of the Agreement, 
including an increase to the Cross Default Threshold Amounts to reflect changes in 
market conditions and the parties' credit profiles;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual agreements contained herein and for 
other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are 
hereby acknowledged, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE I
AMENDMENTS TO CROSS DEFAULT PROVISIONS

Section 1.1 Cross Default Threshold Amount - Superseding Prior Amendment

This Section 1.1 supersedes and replaces in its entirety Section 1.1 of the First 
Amendment dated March 1, 2024.

Part 1(f) of the Schedule to the Agreement is hereby amended and restated in its 
entirety to read as follows:

"(f) Cross Default. The provisions of Section 5(a)(vi) will apply to Party A and 
     will apply to Party B.

     'Specified Indebtedness' means any obligation (whether present or future, 
     contingent or otherwise, as principal or surety or otherwise) in respect of 
     borrowed money, excluding any indebtedness owed to any affiliate of the 
     relevant party.

     'Threshold Amount' means:

     (i) With respect to Party A: USD 25,000,000 (Twenty-Five Million United States 
         Dollars), or if higher, an amount equal to 3% of the shareholders' equity 
         of Party A as shown in its most recent audited financial statements.

     (ii) With respect to Party B: USD 15,000,000 (Fifteen Million United States 
          Dollars)

     'Specified Entities' means:

     (i) With respect to Party A: Barclays PLC and any subsidiary of Barclays PLC 
         that is a Material Subsidiary (as defined in the credit agreement of 
         Barclays PLC).

     (ii) With respect to Party B: World Omni Financial Corp. and any issuer of 
          securities in a securitization transaction sponsored by World Omni 
          Financial Corp.

     For the avoidance of doubt, (a) a default, event of default or other similar 
     condition or event in respect of Specified Indebtedness of a party or any 
     Specified Entity of that party in an aggregate amount equal to or exceeding 
     the applicable Threshold Amount shall constitute a Cross Default under 
     Section 5(a)(vi) of this Agreement, and (b) the Threshold Amount applicable 
     to Party A shall be recalculated annually upon the filing of Party A's 
     audited financial statements."

ARTICLE II
ADDITIONAL TERMINATION EVENT

Section 2.1 Additional Termination Event

The following provision is hereby added to Part 1(k) of the Schedule:

"(k) Additional Termination Event. The following shall constitute an Additional 
     Termination Event:

     (i) Rating Downgrade Event. If at any time:

         (A) With respect to Party A: The long-term senior unsecured debt rating 
             of Party A falls below BBB- by Standard & Poor's or Baa3 by Moody's 
             Investors Service, or either rating agency withdraws its rating of 
             Party A; or

         (B) With respect to Party B: The rating of any class of notes issued by 
             Party B falls below BBB- by Standard & Poor's or Baa3 by Moody's 
             Investors Service;

         then such event shall constitute an Additional Termination Event with 
         respect to the relevant party as the sole Affected Party, and all 
         Transactions shall be Affected Transactions.

     (ii) Net Asset Value Decline. With respect to Party B only: If the Net Asset 
          Value of Party B declines by more than 25% from the Net Asset Value as 
          of the date of this Second Amendment, as determined by Party A acting 
          in good faith, such decline shall constitute an Additional Termination 
          Event with respect to Party B as the sole Affected Party."

ARTICLE III
COLLATERAL REQUIREMENTS

Section 3.1 Credit Support

The following provision is hereby added to Part 4 of the Schedule:

"Credit Support Provider.

(a) Credit Support Provider with respect to Party A: None, unless Party A's 
    long-term senior unsecured debt rating falls below A- by Standard & Poor's 
    or A3 by Moody's, in which case Barclays PLC shall provide a guarantee 
    substantially in the form of the ISDA 2016 Guarantee.

(b) Credit Support Provider with respect to Party B: World Omni Financial Corp.

Credit Support Document.

(a) Credit Support Document with respect to Party A: The guarantee from Barclays 
    PLC, if required pursuant to the above provision.

(b) Credit Support Document with respect to Party B: The Credit Support Annex 
    between the parties, if any, and the guarantee from World Omni Financial Corp."

ARTICLE IV
REPRESENTATIONS, WARRANTIES AND EFFECTIVENESS

Section 4.1 Representations and Warranties

Each party represents and warrants to the other that:

(a) It has all necessary power and authority to execute and deliver this Second 
    Amendment and to perform its obligations hereunder;

(b) This Second Amendment has been duly authorized, executed and delivered by it 
    and constitutes its legal, valid and binding obligation;

(c) No Event of Default, Potential Event of Default, or Termination Event has 
    occurred and is continuing;

(d) All representations and warranties made by it in the Agreement (as amended 
    through the First Amendment) are true and correct as of the date hereof.

Section 4.2 Acknowledgment of Prior Amendments

The parties acknowledge that this Second Amendment is entered into with full 
knowledge of and subject to the First Amendment dated March 1, 2024, and that 
to the extent any provision of this Second Amendment conflicts with or supersedes 
any provision of the First Amendment, this Second Amendment shall control.

Section 4.3 Effectiveness

This Second Amendment shall become effective as of September 15, 2024 (the "Second 
Amendment Effective Date").

Section 4.4 Continuity of Agreement

Except as expressly amended hereby, the Agreement (as amended by the First Amendment 
and this Second Amendment) shall continue in full force and effect in accordance 
with its terms. On and after the Second Amendment Effective Date, each reference 
in the Agreement to "this Agreement," "hereunder," "hereof," or words of like import 
shall mean and be a reference to the Agreement as amended by the First Amendment 
and this Second Amendment.

Section 4.5 Amendment Hierarchy

For the avoidance of doubt, in the event of any conflict between the terms of the 
original Agreement dated September 26, 2007, the First Amendment dated March 1, 2024, 
and this Second Amendment, the following order of precedence shall apply (with 
later documents prevailing over earlier documents):

1. This Second Amendment (highest priority)
2. The First Amendment dated March 1, 2024
3. The original ISDA Master Agreement dated September 26, 2007 (lowest priority)

Section 4.6 Governing Law

This Second Amendment shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the 
laws of the State of New York (without reference to choice of law doctrine).

Section 4.7 Counterparts

This Second Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be 
deemed an original.
